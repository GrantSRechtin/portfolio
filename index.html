Magfit Prosthetics as a independent student research project two other students and I are taking on with the goal of developing a automatic fit-adjusting prosthetic socket using magneto fluidic materials. I'm personally working on designing and developing a sensing apparatus to monitor and map pressure within prosthetic socket along with software using pressure data to control and adjust the fit.
